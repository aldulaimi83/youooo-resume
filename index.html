Resume Tips

How to make it stronger

Lead with action verbs

Use verbs like built, improved, automated, reduced, validated, delivered.

Show results

Whenever possible, add numbers, percentages, time savings, or scale.

Match the JD naturally

Use important keywords honestly where they reflect real experience.

Keep formatting clean

Simple layouts usually work better with applicant tracking systems.
